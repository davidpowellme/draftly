Day 01: Jack Kerouac

Write
"Composing wild, undisciplined, pure, coming in from under, crazier the better"

Tips from Kerouac

  • No periods separating sentence-structures already arbitrarily riddled by false colons and timid usually needless commas
  • No pause to think of proper word but the infantile pileup of scatological buildup words till satisfaction is gained
  • Be crazy dumbsaint of the mind
  • Write in recollection and amazement for yourself
  • Struggle to sketch the flow that already exists intact in mind
  • write as deeply, fish as far down as you want, satisfy yourself first, then reader cannot fail to receive telepathic shock and meaning-excitement by same laws operating in his own human mind
